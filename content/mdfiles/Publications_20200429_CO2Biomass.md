### Direct-Air CO₂ Capture in Biomass

In the paper [**Physical adsorption of CO₂ in biomass at atmospheric pressure and ambient temperature**](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10311-020-01011-y), we showed that CO₂ adsorption is controlled by a **CO₂–water–biomass network** involving the sharing of common water molecules, leading to **5–56 g of CO₂ adsorption per gram of biomass at 230–310 K and atmospheric pressure**.

### Publication: TEMPO-Oxidation of Native Cellulose Molecules

In the paper [**A molecular scale analysis of TEMPO-oxidation of native cellulose molecules**](https://www.cell.com/heliyon/fulltext/S2405-8440(20)32619-0), we addressed that TEMPO-oxidized cellulose accommodates a **threefold screw conformation where the negatively charged (–COO⁻) functional groups are pointed away from the surface in all spatial directions**.

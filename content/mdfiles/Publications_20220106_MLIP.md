### Machine Learning Interatomic Potentials for Nanohardness Prediction

In the paper [**Nanohardness from First Principles with Active Learning on Atomic Environments**](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jctc.1c00783), we develop **machine learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs)** for **nanohardness prediction** in various materials using **density functional theory (DFT)** data and **active learning**.

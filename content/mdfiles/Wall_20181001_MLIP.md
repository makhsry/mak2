### Presentation: Nanohardness from First Principles with Active Learning on Atomic Environments

In **November 2018**, presented **"Nanohardness from First Principles with Active Learning on Atomic Environments"** at [**Max-Planck-Institut für Eisenforschung**](https://www.mpie.de/), Düsseldorf, Germany.

- [**MLIP**](https://gitlab.com/ashapeev/mlip-3) is an open-source package for developing and using **machine learning interatomic potentials** based on the [**Moment Tensor Potentials (MTP)**](https://gitlab.com/ashapeev/mlip-3).

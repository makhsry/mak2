### Raman Spectroscopy Enabled Molecular Scale Targeting of Cocrystals in Continuous Pharmaceutical Cocrystallization

In the paper [**A molecularly enhanced proof of concept for targeting cocrystals at molecular scale in continuous pharmaceuticals cocrystallization**](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2114277119), we demonstrated that **Raman spectroscopy** enables **molecular scale targeting** of **cocrystals** in **continuous pharmaceutical cocrystallization** by analyzing the **vibrational modes** of the molecules to identify the formation of **cocrystals**.

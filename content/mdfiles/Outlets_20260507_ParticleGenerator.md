### Particle Generator and Mesher

An **interactive browser-based tool** for **particle-filled geometry generation** with different **shapes** and **sizes** based on the **particle size distribution (PSD)** and **packing characteristics**. It can generate **3D granular beds** with various shapes and sizes, and **volume meshing**.

- **Access** the tool [**here**](tools/ParticleGenerator.html).

### Molecular Level Analysis of Cocrystal Formation

In the paper [**Molecular engineering of cocrystallization process in holt melt extrusion based on kinetics of elementary molecular processes**](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0378517321003008), we found that **operating at low temperatures (especially lower or very close to the melting temperature of ibuprofen) for longer residency times** creates a safe route for **maximizing the presence of ibuprofen and nicotinamide co-crystals**.

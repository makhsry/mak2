### Kashmir Singh Manhas Scholarship in Applied Science (2023)

Awarded a total of **2,700 CAD** in **2023** by [**The University of British Columbia**](https://www.ubc.ca/) on the recommendation of the [**Faculty of Applied Science**](https://apsc.ubc.ca/), in consultation with the [**Faculty of Graduate and Postdoctoral Studies**](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/), in recognition of **academic excellence**.

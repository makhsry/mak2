Awarded a total of **1,675.00 CAD** in 2023 by [**The University of British Columbia**](https://www.ubc.ca/) on the recommendation of the [**Faculty of Applied Science**](https://apsc.ubc.ca/), in consultation with the [**Faculty of Graduate and Postdoctoral Studies**](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/), in recognition of academic excellence.
